Swimming

Our heated 50-meter Olympic-size pool is among the finest in Orange County. Ideal for lap swimming, cross-training, lessons, or a refreshing workout in the sun.

50-Meter Olympic Pool

Heated year-round for comfortable swimming in any season.

Nova Swim School

Professional swim lessons for children and adults at all skill levels.

Aqua Fitness Classes

Low-impact water-based fitness classes included with membership.

6-8 Water Polo Academy

Structured youth water polo development for players ages 6–8.

Wading & Specialty Pools

Basketball

Los Cab features a full-size indoor basketball court that converts to two half-courts. Open gym is available daily, with organized programs led by Coach Beez.

Full-Size Indoor Court

Converts to 2 half-courts for simultaneous games. Climate-controlled facility.

Daily Open Gym

Open gym available each morning and evening. Note: closed Mon–Fri 3–9 PM for volleyball.

Coached by Coach Beez

Organized basketball programs, drills, and skills sessions with experienced coaching.
