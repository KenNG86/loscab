Happy, Healthy & Active

World-Class Facilities, Community Feel

More than a gym — Los Cab is a community where athletes, families, and weekend warriors all feel at home.

The Pool

Olympic-size 50-meter heated pool. Perfect for lap swimming, cross-training, swim lessons, or a refreshing dip.

Premium Locker Rooms

Each locker room is equipped with spacious showers, steam & sauna rooms, and a co-ed jacuzzi.

Cabanas & Lounge

Semi-private cabanas with showers, lockers, fridge, and TV nestled in the tennis courtyard.

Sol Vida Café

Full-service café offering coffee, smoothies, salads, sandwiches, bagels, and more.
