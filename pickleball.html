World-Class Courts

The Premier Pickleball Facility in Orange County

With 59 total courts — more than any other club in OC — Los Cab is the destination for serious pickleball players.

New Stadium Lighting

All 59 courts feature brand-new lighting systems for perfect visibility during evening sessions.

Dedicated Pickleball Courts

43 courts built exclusively for pickleball — optimal court dimensions, surfacing, and net heights.

Hybrid Courts

16 additional courts convertible for both tennis and pickleball — maximizing your court availability.
