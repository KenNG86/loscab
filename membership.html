FAQ

Common Questions

How do I sign up? ▾
Memberships are processed through Club Automation, our online membership management system. You can also visit us in person at 17272 Newhope St., Fountain Valley or call (714) 546-8560.
Are there long-term contracts? ▾
All memberships are billed monthly. There are no long-term contract requirements. An initiation fee applies when you first join. Contact us for cancellation terms.
Can I try the club before joining? ▾
Yes! Contact us at (714) 546-8560 or visit the club to arrange a tour and trial visit. Our membership team will walk you through all the facilities and programs available.
Are group fitness classes included? ▾
Yes — all group fitness classes (yoga, Pilates, HIIT, Barre, cycling, Fitdanz, line dancing, and more) are included with every Sports, Pickleball, and Tennis membership at no extra charge.
Is personal training extra? ▾
Personal training sessions with our certified trainers are available for an additional fee beyond your monthly membership. Contact the fitness team at fitness@loscab.com to schedule a session.
Can children join? ▾
Absolutely. We offer junior programs for tennis, pickleball, swimming (Nova Swim School), water polo, and more. Family add-on rates allow you to include dependents on your primary membership.

Ready to Start?

Contact us today and our membership team will help you find the perfect plan.
