What's New at Los Cab — Winter 2026 Facility Upgrades

We're excited to share several major upgrades to your Los Cab experience this winter. From brand-new steam and sauna rooms to a dedicated Pilates studio, we've invested in making your club even better.

  • ✦ The "Cool" Pool — new specialty pool added
  • ✦ New dedicated Pilates Room with reformers
  • ✦ Personal Training program relaunched
