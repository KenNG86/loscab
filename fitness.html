Multi-Level Gym

Fitness & Group Classes

A full-featured gym with cardio, free weights, resistance machines, and an expansive group fitness studio for yoga, Pilates, HIIT, and more.

The Gym

A Gym Built for Real Results

Los Cab's multi-level fitness center is equipped with everything you need — from cardio machines to free weights — in a spacious, well-maintained environment.

Cardio Floor

Treadmills, ellipticals, bikes, rowing machines, and stair climbers for every cardio preference.

Free Weights & Resistance Machines

A full range of dumbbells, barbells, cable machines, benches, and resistance equipment for strength training.

Dedicated Group Fitness Studio

A spacious studio for yoga, Pilates, Barre, TRX, and all group classes — with specialized flooring and mirrors.

New Pilates Room

A brand-new dedicated Pilates room added in Winter 2026 — featuring reformers and specialized Pilates equipment.

Personal Training

Relaunched personal training program with certified trainers. One-on-one sessions available for all fitness levels.
